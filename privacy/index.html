Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026  |  Pure Play Rentals LLC  |  Chicago, Illinois

1. Who We Are

Pure Play Rentals LLC ("Pure Play Rentals," "we," "us," or "our") operates pureplayrentals.com and provides bounce house and inflatable rental services throughout Chicago and Chicagoland, Illinois. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or book our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Submit a booking form (name, email, phone number, delivery address)
  • Contact us by phone, text, or email
  • Sign our liability waiver

We may also collect non-personal technical data such as browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs through standard web analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and confirm your rental booking
  • Contact you regarding delivery details, scheduling, and your event
  • Send booking confirmations and receipts
  • Respond to inquiries and customer service requests
  • Improve our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Booking data is stored securely using Supabase (supabase.com), a third-party database provider. Data is stored on servers in the United States. We retain booking records for up to 3 years for business records and legal compliance purposes.

5. Payment Information

We do not store credit card numbers or full payment card details on our servers. Payment processing is handled securely. You should only enter payment details on pages with a secure HTTPS connection.

6. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies for functionality purposes (such as remembering your session). We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (such as Google Maps or review platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for adults booking services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. We will respond to such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
