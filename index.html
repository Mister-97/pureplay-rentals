Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking 1–2 weeks ahead, especially for summer weekends. We do take last-minute bookings — just call us!
Is delivery and setup included?
Yes! Delivery, setup, and takedown are all included within 20 miles. We arrive 60 minutes early so everything is ready before your guests show up.
What if it rains on my party day?
We offer a free weather reschedule if severe weather is forecasted. No stress — we'll find you a new date at no extra charge.
How clean are the inflatables?
Every unit is thoroughly cleaned and disinfected with child-safe, hospital-grade cleaners after every single rental. You can inspect before we leave.
Can I customize the banner text?
Absolutely! When you book, choose "Custom Message" in Step 2 and type whatever you'd like. Up to 40 characters. We print and attach it before delivery.
