FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking 1–2 weeks in advance, especially for summer weekends. We do accommodate last-minute requests when available — just give us a call.
Is delivery and setup included in the price?
Yes — delivery, setup, and takedown are included for locations within 20 miles. We arrive 60 minutes early so everything is ready before guests arrive.
What if it rains on my party day?
We offer a free weather reschedule if severe weather is forecasted. No extra charge — we'll find a new date that works for you.
How clean are the inflatables?
Every unit is cleaned and disinfected with child-safe, hospital-grade products after every single rental. You're welcome to inspect the unit when we arrive.
Can I use a custom banner message?
Absolutely. Select "Custom Message" in Step 2 of the booking flow and type up to 40 characters. We print and attach the banner before delivery.
