Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your party.

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 1–2 weeks in advance, especially for weekends in the summer. That said, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests — just give us a call!
Is delivery and setup included in the price?
Yes! Delivery, setup, and takedown are included for locations within 20 miles. For locations beyond 20 miles, a small travel fee may apply. We'll always be upfront about any extra charges before you confirm.
What if it rains on my party day?
We offer a free weather reschedule! If severe weather is forecasted (high winds or heavy rain), we'll work with you to find a new date at no extra cost. Light sprinkles are usually fine — just keep the entrance dry.
How clean are the inflatables?
Every inflatable is thoroughly cleaned and disinfected with child-safe, hospital-grade cleaners after every single use. We take safety and hygiene very seriously — you can inspect the unit when we arrive and we guarantee your satisfaction.
What size space do I need?
Each listing shows the inflatable's footprint. As a rule, add 4–5 feet on each side for safety clearance, and make sure the area is flat and free of debris. Have overhead obstructions? Let us know and we'll help find the right fit.
Do you require a deposit to hold my date?
We require a 25% deposit to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due on the day of delivery. We accept all major credit cards, Venmo, and cash.
