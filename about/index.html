Who We Are

Pure Play Rentals LLC is a family-owned and operated bounce house and inflatable party rental company based in Chicago, Illinois. We serve families, schools, churches, and community organizations throughout Chicago and the greater Chicagoland area — from Naperville to Schaumburg, Aurora to Tinley Park, and everywhere in between.

We started Pure Play Rentals because we believe birthday parties and community events should be memorable — not stressful. When you book with us, you don't have to worry about a thing. We show up, we set up, we take down. You just show up and party.
