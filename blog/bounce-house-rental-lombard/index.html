Backyard Parties in Lombard

Lombard's residential neighborhoods are some of the most pleasant in DuPage County — a mix of postwar colonials, ranch homes, and newer construction on well-sized lots with mature landscaping. Most Lombard backyards have ample space for a standard or combo bounce house unit, and the village's grid-based street plan means rear alley access is common, making delivery especially smooth.

Spring in Lombard is gorgeous, and backyard parties during Lilac Time (typically the first two weeks of May) are a wonderful tradition for local families. Book early if you're planning a late April or May event — this is a popular time for outdoor gatherings and our calendar fills up. Summer weekends in June through August are also very popular.

Lilac Time and Spring Parties

May is a magical time for outdoor parties in Lombard. The lilac blossoms along the Lilacia Park area create a backdrop that makes outdoor celebrations feel particularly special. If you're planning a spring birthday party during Lilac Time weekend (the festival typically draws large crowds to downtown Lombard), keep in mind that parking and traffic in the downtown area can be congested. Backyard parties in residential neighborhoods are well away from the festival footprint and won't be affected.

A May bounce house party in a Lombard backyard surrounded by blooming lilacs and spring green grass? That's a memory the kids will keep for a long time.

Lombard Park District Options

The Lombard Park District manages a variety of parks and facilities across the village. Madison Meadow Park, Sunset Knoll Recreation Center, and Terrace View Park all have open areas suitable for outdoor gatherings. Park permit applications go through the LPD — apply 4–6 weeks in advance for summer weekends. Confirm power availability at your chosen shelter when you book.

Lilacia Park itself is managed by the Lombard Park District — it's primarily a botanical/ceremonial garden and isn't typically available for private events, but the surrounding areas provide beautiful outdoor party settings.
