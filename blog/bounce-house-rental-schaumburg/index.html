Schaumburg is one of the most bustling family communities in the northwest suburbs, and it's a place where backyard birthday parties are a real tradition. From the quiet residential streets near Woodfield Mall to the newer developments on the outskirts, Schaumburg families know how to make a party memorable — and Pure Play Rentals makes it easy to add a bounce house to the mix.

Backyard Parties in Schaumburg

Schaumburg's residential neighborhoods are full of well-sized backyards — particularly in the subdivisions that were built in the 1980s and 1990s. Most lots have plenty of room for a standard 15×15 or 20×20 bounce house, and the majority of homes have outdoor outlets accessible from the garage or side of the house.

Our setup process is fast and clean. We arrive 60 minutes before your party starts, set up the inflatable, run the blower, confirm everything is secure, and walk you through the safety rules. At the end of your rental window, we return and take everything down. You focus on the party — we handle the rest.

Park Options in Schaumburg

The Schaumburg Park District operates over 50 parks throughout the village, many with reservable pavilions and open grassy areas. Spring Valley Nature Center area parks, Volkening Lake, and Bison Prairie are popular for larger outdoor gatherings. For inflatable rentals at park locations, you'll need a Schaumburg Park District permit — apply through their online portal well in advance of your event date.

The Schaumburg Park District is generally accommodating for inflatable rental permits, but confirm the specific park's rules when you apply. Some parks prohibit stakes in the ground (which means we'd use sandbags instead — not a problem, just let us know in advance).

What to Know Before Booking

  • Lead time: Book 2–4 weeks in advance for spring/fall weekends; 4–6 weeks for summer Saturdays
  • Power: Outdoor outlet within 50 feet of setup, or arrange a generator for park locations
  • Surface: Flat grass, concrete, or asphalt — no hills or uneven terrain
  • Clearance: No overhead power lines or branches within 15 feet of the inflatable's height
