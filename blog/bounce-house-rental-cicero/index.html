Cicero is one of the most densely populated communities in the Chicago metro area, sitting directly west of the city limits and home to a large, vibrant, family-oriented population. Backyard birthday parties are a genuine institution in Cicero's residential neighborhoods, and Pure Play Rentals is proud to deliver bounce houses to Cicero families at the same flat $300 rate we charge everywhere in Chicagoland.

Delivering to Cicero

We serve all of Cicero (60804) and neighboring communities including Berwyn, Stickney, Forest View, and the bordering Chicago neighborhoods of Austin and Little Village. Our flat $300 rate applies across this entire corridor — no extra charge for Cicero's location close to Chicago.

Backyard Parties in Cicero

Cicero's residential landscape is characterized by older two-flats, bungalows, and single-family homes on compact but serviceable lots. Many Cicero backyards have paved or concrete surfaces instead of grass — which is not a problem for bounce house setup. We can anchor inflatables on concrete using sandbags instead of stakes, providing the same secure, safe setup as on grass.

Cicero properties often have alley access at the rear of the property, which is ideal for delivery. Our crew can bring the inflatable directly through the alley to the backyard, minimizing disruption to the front of the house. If your property has a narrow alley or specific access considerations, just mention it when you book.

For smaller Cicero yards, our compact standard bounce house (roughly 15×15 ft footprint when inflated) fits comfortably without overwhelming the space. We'll confirm the dimensions match your setup area before your party day.

Community Events in Cicero

Cicero hosts a vibrant annual community calendar, particularly in the summer months when block parties, quinceañeras, and large family gatherings fill up backyards and alley spaces across the town. Pure Play regularly serves parties in Cicero during these busy summer months. If you're planning a larger event and need the inflatable available for an extended window, let us know when you book — we'll accommodate the timeline.

For park events in the Cicero/Berwyn/Stickney area, the Cook County Forest Preserves offer open space at nearby Waterfall Glen and Des Plaines River Trail area groves. These require Cook County Forest Preserve permits for private events.
