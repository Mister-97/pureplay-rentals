Backyard Parties in Tinley Park

Tinley Park's residential neighborhoods are a bounce house company's dream. The village's ranches, split-levels, and newer two-story homes sit on lots that almost universally have enough backyard space for an inflatable. Most homes have rear yard access through a side gate, and outdoor outlets are standard on Tinley Park properties of every age.

Our crew navigates residential properties professionally — we take care not to damage landscaping during setup and teardown, and we always clean up after ourselves. If you have particular concerns about your yard (soft soil, a decorative garden near the setup area, etc.), just let us know when you call and we'll discuss the best approach.

Tinley Park Park District

The Tinley Park Park District manages parks and recreation facilities throughout the village. Candle Creek Woods, Volunteer Park, and Heritage Park are among the most popular open spaces. Pavilion reservations are available online through the TPPD, and the district is generally accommodating about bounce house rentals with advance notice and a proper permit application.

Summer weekends at Tinley Park parks are popular — particularly for birthday parties and family reunions. Book your park shelter 4–6 weeks in advance for summer Saturdays. Confirm power availability at the shelter when you reserve.
