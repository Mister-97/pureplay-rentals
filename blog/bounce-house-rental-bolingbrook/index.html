Riverview Farm Park and Bolingbrook Parks

One of the most popular outdoor party destinations in Bolingbrook is Riverview Farm Park, an open green space near the heart of the village that offers a pastoral setting for outdoor gatherings. The Bolingbrook Park District manages numerous parks throughout the village, many with reservable shelter areas.

For bounce house parties at park locations, submit a permit application to the Bolingbrook Park District in advance. Summer weekend slots at popular parks go quickly — apply 4–6 weeks before your event. Some shelters have electrical power on-site; confirm this when you reserve, or plan to rent a generator.

Bolingbrook's Promenade area also hosts community events throughout the summer — if you're planning a large family gathering in the area, it's worth checking whether any public events fall on your date, as parking and traffic can be affected.
