Arlington Heights Backyard Parties

Arlington Heights' residential neighborhoods are a mix of post-war ranch homes, split-levels, and newer construction — and most have perfectly serviceable backyards for a bounce house party. The typical lot size in Arlington Heights provides enough space for a standard bounce house (15×15 or 20×20 ft) with room to spare for lawn chairs and a picnic table.

Access is generally straightforward — most properties have side gates or rear alley access that our delivery crew can navigate without difficulty. If your property has a narrow gate or any unusual access considerations, let us know when you book and we'll plan accordingly.

Arlington Heights Park District

The Arlington Heights Park District manages an excellent network of parks including Pioneer Park, North School Park, and Camelot Park. Pavilion reservations are available online through the AHPD website. Summer weekend slots book quickly — especially for pavilions with flat grass areas adjacent to them, which are ideal for inflatable setup.

When applying for a park permit that includes an inflatable rental, mention it on the application. Most AHPD parks accommodate this with advance notice. Confirm whether electrical power is available at the specific pavilion, or plan to bring a generator.
