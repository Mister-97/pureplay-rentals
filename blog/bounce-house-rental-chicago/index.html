Backyard Parties in the City

Chicago bungalow and two-flat backyards are often smaller than suburban yards, but many are still perfectly sized for a standard bounce house. Our most popular city unit has a 15×15 ft footprint — compact enough for tighter Chicago lots but still big enough for a great time. We'll confirm dimensions with you before delivery so there are no surprises.

The main requirements: a flat surface (grass, concrete, or asphalt all work), an outdoor electrical outlet within 50 feet, no overhead power lines or tree branches in the setup area, and clear gate access for our delivery team. Most Chicago backyards meet these criteria without any problem.

Park Rentals in Chicago

Chicago Park District parks are a popular option for families who want more space or a more festive open-air setting. We frequently deliver to CPD parks across the city. Important note: you'll need a Chicago Park District permit before we can set up at a city park. The CPD permit portal is online, and applications typically take 5–10 business days to process. Some parks also require proof of liability insurance for inflatable equipment — we have documentation ready to share when needed.
