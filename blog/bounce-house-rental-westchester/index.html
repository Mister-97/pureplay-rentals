5 min read  ·  Pure Play Rentals  ·  Chicago, IL  ·  March 22, 2026

Westchester is a small, tight-knit suburb just west of Chicago that punches well above its size when it comes to community spirit. Sandwiched between Broadview, Bellwood, and Hillside, this village of roughly 17,000 residents has a strong family culture — and Pure Play Rentals is happy to bring bounce house fun right to Westchester's doorsteps at a flat $300 rate.

Serving Westchester and the Surrounding Area

Pure Play delivers to all of Westchester (60154) and neighboring communities including Broadview, Bellwood, Hillside, Maywood, and Melrose Park. Our flat $300 rate applies to the entire area — no surcharge for being west of Chicago.

Backyard Parties in Westchester

Westchester's residential streets are classic Midwest suburb — modest, well-maintained homes on appropriately sized lots. Many backyards have enough space for a standard bounce house, and the community's solid older housing stock means outdoor outlets and gate access are generally available without issue.

If your backyard is on the smaller side, we'll recommend a compact unit that maximizes fun without requiring a massive footprint. Our standard bounce house works in spaces as small as 18×18 feet with adequate overhead clearance. Just describe your yard when you call and we'll match you with the right inflatable.

Community and Park Events in Westchester

Westchester's park system, while smaller than larger suburbs, provides some nice options for outdoor gatherings. The village parks department can advise on permit requirements for events that include inflatable equipment. For many Westchester families, the backyard is the preferred party venue — it's more private, more convenient, and you have everything you need right at home.

Westchester is also close enough to Brookfield's community park spaces and to Maywood Park District facilities for families who want a bigger open area for a party. If you're considering a park location outside Westchester, we'll still deliver — our service area covers all these surrounding towns.
