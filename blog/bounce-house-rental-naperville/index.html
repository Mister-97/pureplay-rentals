Popular Party Spots in Naperville

Naperville families have two great options for bounce house parties: backyards and parks. The city's newer subdivisions typically have larger lots with plenty of space for a standard or combo bounce unit. Backyards are the most popular choice because you have a bathroom nearby and full control over the space.

For park parties, the Naperville Park District manages a fantastic system of parks with reservable shelters. Centennial Park near downtown, Knoch Knolls Park on the south end, and Frontier Sports Complex on the north side all have flat areas suitable for inflatable setup. Book your park shelter reservation through the Naperville Park District website — popular summer weekend slots fill up months in advance, so plan ahead.

Another popular option for Naperville summer parties: the area around Centennial Beach. While the beach itself doesn't allow private inflatables, the surrounding park areas and nearby neighborhoods make it easy to combine a pool/beach morning with a backyard bounce house party in the afternoon.
