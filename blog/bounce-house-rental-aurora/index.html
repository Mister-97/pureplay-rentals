Bounce House Rentals in Aurora, IL — Chicago's Largest Suburb

6 min read  ·  Pure Play Rentals  ·  Chicago, IL  ·  March 6, 2026

Aurora is the second-largest city in Illinois — larger than many people realize — and its diverse, family-oriented communities make it one of the most active markets for bounce house rentals in the Chicagoland area. Whether you're in the Fox Valley neighborhoods to the west, the newer developments near Route 59 and Eola Road, or the established communities closer to downtown, Pure Play Rentals serves all of Aurora with the same commitment to quality and transparency.

Fox Valley Area Parties

Aurora's Fox River corridor and Fox Valley neighborhoods are a hotspot for outdoor family gatherings. The area around the Fox Valley Mall, the RiverEdge Park outdoor amphitheater area, and the sprawling residential neighborhoods east and west of the river all offer excellent options for backyard bounce house parties. The lots in Aurora's residential neighborhoods are generally well-sized, with most having room for a standard or combo bounce house unit and plenty of yard space left over for lawn games and picnic tables.

Aurora's Park System

The Aurora Area Convention and Tourism Council and the City of Aurora Parks, Recreation and Cultural Arts Department manage a robust network of parks throughout the city. Many parks have reservable pavilion spaces — perfect for bounce house party setups. Blackberry Farm and Oakhurst Forest Preserve area parks on the west end, and Phillips Park on the east side, are both popular choices for larger family gatherings. Permit applications go through the City of Aurora parks department, and summer weekends go quickly — apply 4–6 weeks out.

What Makes Aurora Great for Bounce House Parties

Aurora's size and layout give party hosts a lot of flexibility. Newer subdivisions in the 60502 and 60503 zip codes (near the Naperville border) typically have newer, well-maintained backyards with easy gate access for delivery crews. Older neighborhoods near downtown Aurora have more varied lot sizes but often have charming outdoor spaces with mature shade trees. We've set up bounce houses in all types of Aurora properties — just tell us what to expect when you book and we'll bring the right equipment.
